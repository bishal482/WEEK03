Kakani

Kakani is a small hill station located about 24 kilometers northwest of Kathmandu, Nepal. It is known for its stunning panoramic views of the Himalayan mountain range and as a peaceful retreat away from the hustle and bustle of the city.

Top Attractions

Panoramic Views of the Himalayas

Kakani is famous for its breathtaking views of the Himalayan ranges, including peaks like Ganesh Himal, Langtang, Annapurna, and Manaslu. On a clear day, the views are absolutely stunning, making it a favorite spot for photographers and nature lovers.

Kakani Memorial Park

This park is a peaceful place built in memory of the Thai Airways crash victims of 1992. It offers a serene environment with greenery, flowers, and places to relax, along with beautiful views of the surrounding hills.

With guided hikes, nature tours, and nearby attractions like Shivapuri Nagarjun National Park, Kakani Hotel is perfect for those seeking relaxation or adventure amidst nature's beauty.

  • Kakani Homestay: Kakani Homestay offers an authentic Nepali experience in the peaceful setting of Kakani hill station. With stunning views of the Himalayas, cozy accommodations, and opportunities for trekking and exploring nearby attractions, Kakani Homestay provides a warm and immersive retreat for nature lovers and cultural enthusiasts.
